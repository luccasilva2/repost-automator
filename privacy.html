Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Repost Automator handles user data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information required to authenticate your TikTok account using the official TikTok API.

2. Access Tokens

OAuth tokens are stored securely and used exclusively to upload videos to TikTok on behalf of the user.

3. No Sharing of Data

Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

4. User Control

You may revoke access at any time through TikTok account settings.

5. Security

We implement measures to protect stored authentication data.

6. Content Handling

Videos uploaded through this service are only published to accounts you explicitly authorize.

For further questions, contact us.
